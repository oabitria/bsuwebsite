The BSU Marketplace is an innovative solution designed to address a critical gap within the Bowie State University (BSU) community. Currently, BSU students do not have a dedicated platform to promote or sell their items. If they do engage in sales, it is often through third-party platforms, which can create barriers to communication, security, and accessibility. The BSU Marketplace will serve as a standalone application that fosters student interaction and enhances campus connectivity, strengthening the university community.
